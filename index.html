SongTrax

SongTrax is an innovative web application designed for music enthusiasts to create and share their songs. This platform allows users to mix tracks, apply effects, and compose music online. Utilizing advanced web technologies such as Web Audio API, React, and Node.js, SongTrax offers a real-time music-making experience. Its features include a virtual mixing desk, a library of loops, samples, and an online community where users can collaborate and showcase their creations. Whether you're a novice or a seasoned musician, SongTrax provides the tools to bring your musical ideas to life.
